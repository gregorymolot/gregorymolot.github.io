About Me

Nice To Meet You!

I have always loved video games. Some of my strongest memories as a child was sitting in my basement and staying up too late to play Ocarina Of Time. When I was younger I just saw them as fun, but growing up I see them for their true potential. Video games are a beautiful and deep medium, capable of telling any story, and creating a rich tapestry for both the player and creator to dive into.

I am a recent graduate of Algonquin College’s Game Development program and I am currently based in Montreal, Quebec. Through this education I have learned not only to program in C++ and C#, but also learned Level Design, 3-D Modelling, writing, and developing games in both Unreal and Unity.

When I’m not working or at school, you’ll most likely find me playing video games, playing DnD, or finding a new project to work on. I always like to keep busy, always looking for something new to dive into and learn.
